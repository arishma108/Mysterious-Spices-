About Us

We here at Mysterious Spices is interactive and specific, enhancing the vibrance in the use of natural spices. We ensure meeting client needs by mixing spices exclusive to customer requests.
We keep up with the rapidly changing dieting trends and demands by adequately supplying appropriate spices.

We pride ourselves on offering a dynamic range of spices produced in accordance with specified "Food Safety" regulations.
Our products are produced in a HACCP certified factory and are free from foreign bodies.
   *All pure spices.
   *No mashed potatoes.
   *No maize meal.
   *Lab approved.
   *CAO Analysis certificate.
We ensure compliance with all relevant legislation and comply with the R918 (Department of Health Regulation for Food Handlers), SANS 10049.

The "Mysterious Spices" range is made up of the unique balance between the unknown and familiar tastes, making it a much needed accompaniment to your dish.
Allow us to tantalize the taste buds of everybody around the table and let our flavours of mystery take control.
